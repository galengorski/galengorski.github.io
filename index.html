Galen Gorski

PhD Candidate

UC Santa Cruz Earth and Planetary Sciences Deparment

















I am interested in issues related to water quantity and quality

in natural and managed landscapes.

I work with Andrew Fisher and the rest of the UCSC Hydrogeology Team

to understand fundemental connections between

hydrologic and biogeochemical cycling.

I use a combination of field, lab and modeling methods,

and I am particularly interested in developing understanding

at multiple different scales from centimeter to continental.
